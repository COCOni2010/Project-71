Campus life

My School

This is my school. It is one of the greatest schools I've ever seen. I love my school. The teachers are so helpful and guiding. The school is co-operative. All the principals which came to our made rules for our comforts and benifits. The last thing I want to say is, This school is awesome.

School Backlawn

In every games period we used to go to the back lawn it really used to be a lot of fun. Sometimes we used to do the fitness with our sports teacher, and on there hand, we used to enjoy ourseleves playing there. We used to play many games. We normally used to play football.

     

School Library

In every Library period we used to go to the library. Sometimes we used to do some activities like making our own bookmarks etc. and sometimes we used to read the books. They also used to allow us to bring our own favourite books from our homes. Mostly I used to bring My favourite book: Wings of Fire.

     



Achievments

Inter School Competiton- Deutsch Jamboree
Won first prize in inter school competition- deutsch jamboree

Four students from K.R. Mangalam World School, Gurugram participated in an Inter School German competition, Deutsch Jamboree where in around 12 schools from Delhi-NCR participated. Students from K.R. Mangalam World School , Gurugram participated in two out of four events- The Wall Hanging Competition and Cosmic Gesehichte(Comic strip Making). Manas Barve from VIII-B and Mia Goel from VII-C bagged the third prize in Comic Geschichte. The topic was 'Ich Bin in Deutschland' – I am in Germany. Navkiran and Maitrisha Gupta from class VI also performed well in the Wall Hanging Making Competition. The school principal Ms Priya Arora congratulated the winners for their achievement.




what's new


  • Assembly-Community Service During COVID 19

    K.R Mangalam World School, Gurugram organized an assembly on ‘Community Service during Covid 19.’ Gayatri Mantra with Hindi and English prayers gave a divine inception to the assembly. The students of class IX C and D steered the assembly by introducing the theme of the assembly and made the virtual audience aware about the recent guidelines of Government during unlock period. They also gave the information about that how can we be safe and healthy during the festive season.



  • Fitness Freak – plank challenge’ – Inter School Sports Fiesta

    ‘Fitness is not just a word but a necessary condition for healthy and properous life’. This effective quote by Honorable Prime Minister: Mr Narendra Modi and his launch of FIT IndiaMovement has surely encouraged the students of K.R Mangalam World School, Gurugram to remain FIT even during these times of global pandemic when they are confined to their homes.Yashita of class XI proved her mettle and bagged second position in ‘Fitness Freak – Plank Challenge’ in Inter School Sports Fiesta organised at Modern School, Faridabad.
